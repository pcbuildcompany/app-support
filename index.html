Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how EV Charge Finder ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect are:

  • Location Data: To provide the core functionality of finding nearby charging stations, the App requests access to your device's location. We use this information to display your position on the map, find nearby stations, and calculate distances. This data is used only while the App is active and is not stored or shared.
  • Advertising Data: Our App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service, to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about you, such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, and information about your interactions with ads, to provide personalized advertising.

How We Use Information

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: We use your location data to power the features of the App.
  • To Show Relevant Advertisements: We partner with Google AdMob to display ads, which helps keep the App free. You can learn more about how Google collects and uses data by visiting their Google Ads Policy page.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with third parties as described in this policy:

  • Service Providers: We share information with Google AdMob for the purpose of serving advertisements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
